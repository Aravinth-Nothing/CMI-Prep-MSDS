Problem 30

Show that, $\frac{\log_a x}{\log_{ab} x}=1+\log_a b$

Solution

$$\frac{\log_a x}{\log_{ab} x}=\frac{\left(\frac{\cancel{\log x}}{\log a}\right)}{\left(\frac{\cancel{\log x}}{\log ab}\right)}=\frac{\log ab}{\log a}=\frac{\log a+\log b}{\log a}$$ $$=1+\frac{\log b}{\log a}$$ $$=1+\log_a b$$

Hence Shown that,
$$\frac{\log_a x}{\log_{ab} x}=1+\log_a b$$
