Problem 26

If $x\neq1$ and $\log_3 x\log_4 x\log_5 x = \log_4 x\log_5 x+\log_3 x\log_5 x+\log_4 x\log_3 x$
then the value of $x$ is,
a) 20
b) 30
c) 10
d) 60

Solution

$$\begin{array} \\p=\log_3 x & q=\log_4 x & r=\log_5 x \end{array}$$ Substituting in the Original Equation,
$$pqr=pq+qr+rp$$ Divide the Equation by $pqr$
$$\frac{1}{p}+\frac{1}{q}+\frac{1}{r}=1$$ $$\frac{1}{\log_3 x}+\frac{1}{\log_4 x}+\frac{1}{\log_5 x}= \log_x 3+\log_x 4+\log_x 5$$ $$=\log_x (3.4.5)=\log_x 60=1$$ $$x=60$$

d) is the Correct Answer.
