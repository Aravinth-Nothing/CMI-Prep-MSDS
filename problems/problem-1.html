Problem 1

Find the number of positive integral solutions of: $$x^4-y^4=3789108$$

Solution

$$x^4-y^4=(x^2-y^2)(x^2+y^2)$$ $$(x^2-y^2)(x^2+y^2)=(x+y)(x-y)(x^2+y^2)=3789108$$ It is evident that $3789108$ is an even number. Therefore both $x $ & $y$ must be odd or both $x $ & $y$ must be even.
In both cases. $(x+y)$ will be divisible by 2, $(x-y)$ will be divisible by 2 and $(x^2+y^2)$ will be divisible by 2.

$\therefore x^4-y^4$ must be divisible by 8.
Checking it: $$\frac{3789108}{8}=\frac{1894554}{4}=\frac{947277}{2} \notin \mathbb{Z}$$ Since $3789108$ is not divisible by 8, This equation has no positive integral solution.
