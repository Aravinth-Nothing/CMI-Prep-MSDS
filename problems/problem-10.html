Problem 10

If $f(\theta)=5\cos\theta+3\cos(\theta+\frac{\pi}{3})+3$, find the range of $f(\theta)$

Solution

Given Equation, $$=5\cos\theta+3\left(\cos\theta\cos\frac{\pi}{3}-\sin\theta\sin\frac{\pi}{3}\right)+3$$ $$=5\cos\theta+\frac{3\cos\theta}{2}-\frac{3\sqrt{3}\sin\theta}{2}+3$$ $$=\frac{13\cos\theta}{2}-\frac{-3\sqrt{3}\sin\theta}{2}+3$$ Note: Range of a function of form: $a\sin x+b\cos x$ is $[-\sqrt{a^2+b^2},\sqrt{a^2+b^2}]$
In this case, $a=\frac{13}{2}, b=\frac{-3\sqrt{3}}{2}$ $$\sqrt{a^2+b^2}=\sqrt{\left(\frac{13}{2}\right)^2+\left(\frac{-3\sqrt{3}}{2}\right)^2}$$ $$=\sqrt{\frac{169+27}{4}}=\sqrt{\frac{196}{4}}=\frac{14}{2}=\pm7$$ Range of $\frac{13\cos\theta}{2}-\frac{-3\sqrt{3}\sin\theta}{2}$ is [-7,7]

$\therefore$ Range of $\frac{13\cos\theta}{2}-\frac{-3\sqrt{3}\sin\theta}{2}+3$ is
$$[-4,10]$$
